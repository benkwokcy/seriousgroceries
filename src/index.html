Cooking your favorite dishes just got cheaper


What's for dinner?

What is this?

Serious Groceries helps you decide what to cook for dinner cheaply.

How?

Every day, it finds recipes that can be shopped for cheaply in grocery stores near you. You can enter your own recipes or use the recommended recipes.

How is this different from Flipp?

Serious Groceries not only tracks flyers but it also finds the best recipes for you to cook.
